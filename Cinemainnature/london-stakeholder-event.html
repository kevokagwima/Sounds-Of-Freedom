About the Event

Join us for a landmark stakeholder event marking the opening of the 2025 UK-Kenya Season of Culture. This gathering will bring together cultural leaders, creators, enterprises, and diplomatic representatives to celebrate our shared heritage and imagine new creative pathways.

Presented as part of the Echoes of Freedom project awarded by the British Council, this event will explore Kenya's fight for independence through immersive storytelling and cross-border collaboration. We'll honor memory, deepen understanding, and reframe historical narratives through innovative audio-visual formats.

This pre-London experience will highlight the importance of collaboration, shared storytelling, and the power of cultural exchange between Kenya, the UK, and the wider diaspora.
