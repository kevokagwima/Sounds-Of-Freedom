What is Echoes of Freedom?

Echoes of Freedom is a collaborative project between Sounds of Freedom Africa (Kenya) and Echoes.xyz (UK), supported by the British Council's Catalyst Grant Follow-On Fund as part of the 2025 UK–Kenya Season of Culture.

Through immersive audio films and curated nature experiences, the project reimagines Kenya's independence struggle, offering audiences in Kenya and the UK a deeply engaging, tech-enabled way to interact with colonial history.

In a time when global conversations around historical injustice are expanding, Echoes of Freedom offers a reflective space for both British and Kenyan communities to come together. It seeks not to reopen wounds, but to open hearts — through story, memory, and meaningful exchange.

The London Showcase

Richmond Park Experience

31 May – 1 June 2025
Richmond Park, London

Join us this Madaraka Day weekend in London for an unforgettable immersive experience in nature. Set against the lush trails of Richmond Park, you'll walk with headphones through a geolocated soundscape that brings Kenya's independence struggle to life.

The featured episode, Dear Colonisers: A Letter from 1955, follows a group of young Mau Mau initiates on their first mission — a daring raid that unveils the emotional weight of resistance and the human cost of freedom.

The event will include:

  • Panel discussions with historians, creatives, and the diaspora
  • Fireside chats on legacy, resistance, and remembrance
  • A special fundraiser to support the creation of a new episode from British settler perspectives
